Join us at the Community Science Museum, where we’re committed to making science accessible to all.

At CSM we've got interesting opportunities for all ages. Wheter you're a student, a teacher or an experienced researcher, we welcome you to explore our museum!

The museum has over 1000 different exhibits on the many varied subjects of science to explore. Many of these exhibits are designed for you to interact with and play around to see science come to life (apart from the dinosaur exhibits – they only come to life at night when everyone’s gone home).

CSM often hosts visiting exhibits as well as live scientist lectures on various topics. This month we're hosting a live version of "StarTalk" with astrophysicist dr. Neil deGrasse Tyson!
